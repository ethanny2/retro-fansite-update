Lana Del Rey ft. A$AP Rocky & Playboi Carti (??)

So I remember this song be hyped up a lot in the months before release. "Wow Carti, Rocky and Lana on one track that sounds like a banger!" was the sentiment. But it looks like it this song pulled another "Raf" on us. Carti has no verses in the song other than his typical "What, What, What" ad-libs. I starting to wonder if Carti originally had verses on these songs ('Raf' and 'Summer Bummer'), but they cut him in the end. OR, they really wanted a more lyrical artist on the song but they use Carti's name and ad-libs to get more people interested in the song. Honestly that's a little unnecessary, but that's just my opinion. I actually like the mix of Lana's vocals with Rockys verse, no need to slap Carti on there if he doesn't have a verse.
